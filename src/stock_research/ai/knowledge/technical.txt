Technical analysis focuses on studying price movements and trading volumes to identify patterns and trends. Key concepts include:

1. Trend Analysis:
- Primary, Secondary, and Minor Trends
- Support and Resistance Levels
- Trendlines and Channels
- Price Patterns (Head & Shoulders, Double Tops/Bottoms)

2. Moving Averages:
- Simple Moving Average (SMA)
- Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
- Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
- Golden Cross and Death Cross

3. Momentum Indicators:
- Relative Strength Index (RSI)
- Stochastic Oscillator
- Rate of Change (ROC)
- Money Flow Index (MFI)

4. Volume Analysis:
- Volume Trends
- On-Balance Volume (OBV)
- Volume Price Trend (VPT)
- Accumulation/Distribution Line

Consider multiple timeframes and confirm signals using different indicators. Look for:
- Trend confirmation
- Volume confirmation
- Divergences
- Overbought/Oversold conditions
- Key level breakouts/breakdowns
