Sentiment analysis examines market psychology and public perception through news, social media, and other sources. Key aspects include:

1. News Analysis:
- Major News Events
- Press Releases
- Earnings Reports
- Industry News
- Regulatory Changes

2. Social Media Sentiment:
- Twitter Mentions
- Reddit Discussion
- StockTwits Activity
- Social Media Trends

3. Market Sentiment Indicators:
- Fear & Greed Index
- Put/Call Ratio
- VIX (Volatility Index)
- Short Interest

4. Institutional Activity:
- Insider Trading
- Institutional Ownership
- Analyst Coverage
- Short Selling Activity

Consider the reliability of sources and overall market context. Look for:
- Sentiment shifts
- Consensus views
- Contrarian indicators
- Market positioning
- Information credibility
- Source reliability
